[Zeabur](https://zeabur.com/ "Zeabur - Your AI DevOps Engineer") is a platform you can use. Although it's not quite "Plug In And Play," the setup is simple.

### Make an account

1. - Go to [Zeabur](https://zeabur.com/dashboard "Zeabur - Your AI DevOps Engineer"); this will prompt you to make an account.
- If you have a [Google](https://google.com "It's Simply GOOGLE") account, press **"Sign In With Google"** — it should look like this:

![Sign in with Google](../../Assets/signin-google.png)

   Alternatively, use your [GitHub Account](https://github.com "GitHub :)").
